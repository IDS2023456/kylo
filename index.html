KYLO


Korean Youth Leadership Organization(KYLO) is a high school organization established in 2025. KYLO is established to introduce beginner delegates to MUN by providing an accessible and educational conference that fosters leadership and diplomacy among youth. KYLO believes in the development of young leadeers through Model United Nation (MUN) participation. Recalling their inital experiences in MUN, KYLO members recoginzed the challenges faced by beginner delegates, who may find conferences intimidating and competitive. Thus, KYO envisions a conference aimed at beginner delegates, guiding them towards effective MUN participation.
